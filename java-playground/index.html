Italian Panini

This is a recipe for an italian panini sandwich made from fresh ingredients

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Tomato
  • Pesto
  • Mozzarela
  • Proscuto

Instructions

  1. Toast bread
  2. Cut ingredients
  3. Sread the pesto on each side of the toast
  4. Put all ingredients instide the toast
  5. Cut in the middle
